Welcome to Griddin! Every time Griddin is played, it's a different set of tiles! Easy to play: swap grid tiles to match at least 3 like tiles. Stuck? Use the (?) Hint button. But, watch out! Hints will cost you points. Have some fun with this casual and relaxing game.
